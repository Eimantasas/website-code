Saul Goodman Finally Caught

James Morgan “Jimmy” McGill, better known by his
professional alias and business moniker Saul Goodman,
is an American criminal defence lawyer, scam artist,
and convicted criminal who is serving an 86-year sentence
at ADX Montrose.

Originally from Cicero, Illinois during his career as a Scam
artist, Jimmy moved to Albuquerque, New Mexico where he
worked as a lawyer. During his Law career, Jimmy embraced
his tendencies as a Former scam artist and, after becoming
a dedicated and effective Criminal lawyer, he began to represent
criminals while he himself Became increasingly involved in the
city’s criminal underworld, even Representing “Heisenberg” at
some point.

With the increasing Danger of being caught, Jimmy
Moved to Omaha and changed his name and identity
To Gene Takavic, working as a cinnamon bun store
Manager. This, however, did not last long as he
was Later caught and apprehended in a federal
prison at Montrose, Colorado.
