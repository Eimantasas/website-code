What kind of news do we deliver?

We are a news station which primarily focuses on news about criminal activity.
We work closely with the police and manage which articles should make it to the
Front page. Some information is classified and should not be revealed. We make sure
To inform you of things you should be informed of.

Why Black & Gray Matters?

Most of our news focuses on crimes and criminals range from morally grey, to morally
black. That is the name. The news range from serious crime to really serious crime.

Why news on crime?

We are trying to make a difference in the world by saving lives or giving
Someone an interesting read. We WILL deliver crime news as soon as possible.
Focusing only on that gives us an economical advantage as well as many more
Benefits, such as making our subscription extremely cheap!
